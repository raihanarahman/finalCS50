How to Play

Each square represents a population of animals (like bunnies!). Select different populations of these animals and see how, over the course of multiple generations, they live or die.

There are a few rules to living or dying in this simulation:

  1. Any population with less than two live neighbors dies by underpopulation.
  2. Any population with more than three live neighbors dies by overpopulation.
  3. Any population with two or three neighbors lives!
  4. Any population with three neighbors that is dead can come back to life!

Live populations are in green, and dead populations are red. After selecting the live populations, choose how many generations you want this simulation to run for. Then, hit start to see what happens! After your simulation is done, press restart to try a different pattern of live populations.
